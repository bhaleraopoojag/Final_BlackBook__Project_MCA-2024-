About Us

House price prediction websites provide estimated values of properties based on
        various factors such as location, size, age, and condition. These websites use
        algorithms and historical data to make predictions about the future value of a house.
        Some popular house price prediction websites include Zillow, Trulia, and Redfin.
        These websites can be helpful for home buyers, sellers, and real estate agents to
        better understand the value of a property and make informed decisions.
        It's important to note that house price prediction is not an exact science and
        the estimated values may vary from the actual prices.
